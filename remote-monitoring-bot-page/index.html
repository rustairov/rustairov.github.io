What is it?

RemoteMonitoringBot is a bot that allows you to keep an eye on current state of your GPU rigs. The bot sends you a message to Telegram with all gathered data. By pressing one button, the bot makes it possible to gather such information as: uptime, speed (hashrate), power, temperature, cooler rotation speed, statistics on shares etc (type of gathered information may vary depending on miner type), and sends it all over to you to Telegram chat.

How to connect?

To use the bot, your miner must be available to Internet World Web, therefore you need to set up static IP or use such services as Ngrok. Use any method that suits you.
